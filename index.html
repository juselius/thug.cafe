About THUG

We are a group of Haskell and functional programming enthusiasts who enjoy a friendly discussion about programming languages and philosophy. The group spans the full spectrum from die-hard Haskeller to novice to people who have heard of Haskell and are simply curious to learn more. Our aim is to learn and teach and exchange and have fun.
